Overview

Bach Electrical and Control Instrumentation is a specialist engineering company delivering end-to-end Electrical and Control Instrumentation (EC&I) solutions. From project concept and design to installation, commissioning, and maintenance, we support both industrial operations and residential housing projects with precision, safety, and efficiency.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide innovative, reliable, and safe EC&I solutions that enhance the performance of industrial systems and residential infrastructure.

Vision: To become a leading name in EC&I services across Mali, recognised for technical excellence, customer trust, and consistent delivery.
